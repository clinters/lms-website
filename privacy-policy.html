Privacy Policy

This WhatsApp bot is designed to manage football pitch bookings for West Kirby United Junior Football teams.

Information We Collect

  • Team manager phone numbers
  • Booking requests and confirmations

How We Use Information

We use phone numbers only to link managers with their teams and confirm bookings via WhatsApp. No personal data is shared with third parties.

Data Storage

All booking data is stored securely in Google Sheets and is only accessible to administrators.
